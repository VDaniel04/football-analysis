# ⚽ International Football Data Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes international football matches using Python, focusing on scoring patterns, match balance, and team evolution over time.

Dataset source: Kaggle
(International football results dataset)
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/martj42/international-football-results-from-1872-to-2017

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze goal distribution in football matches
* Identify home advantage
* Detect "exciting" matches (high scoring + balanced)
* Study team dominance vs match balance
* Explore evolution of teams over time

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* Python
* pandas
* matplotlib
* seaborn

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### ⚽ Goal Distribution

* Most matches have between 0–5 goals
* High-scoring matches are rare

---

### 📉 Evolution of Goals

* Average goals per match slightly decreased over time
* Modern football tends to be more tactical and controlled

---

### 🏠 Home Advantage

* Home teams win more frequently
* Confirms known football patterns

---

### 🔥 "Exciting" Matches

* Only ~30% of matches are both high-scoring and balanced
* Truly exciting matches are relatively rare

---

### ⚖️ Match Balance

* Most matches are decided by small goal differences
* Football is generally a low-scoring, balanced sport

---

### 🌍 Team Analysis

* Strong teams tend to dominate rather than produce "exciting" matches
* Mid-level teams produce more balanced, high-scoring games

---

### 📊 Team Evolution (Example: Brazil)

* Consistent offensive performance over time
* Variations explained by:

  * historical context (e.g., WWII)
  * generational changes
  * number of matches played per year

---

## 📌 Conclusions

Football matches are generally balanced and low-scoring.
The most exciting matches tend to occur between teams of similar strength rather than dominant teams.
